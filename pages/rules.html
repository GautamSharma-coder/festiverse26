• All participants must carry their University ID cards at all times.

• Any form of harassment or unruly behavior will lead to immediate disqualification and removal from the premises.

• Alcohol and illegal substances are strictly prohibited inside the "Grand Orion Arena".

• Participants must report to the venue 30 minutes before their slot.

• Judges' decisions are final and binding. No appeals will be entertained.

• For team events, substitutions are only allowed with prior written approval from the Core Committee.

• BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) is mandatory for Hackathon participants unless stated otherwise.

• E-Sports: Use of aimbots, scripts, or any external assistance will result in a permanent ban.

• Stable WiFi will be provided, but having a backup hotspot is recommended.
