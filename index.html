Got Questions?

QUICK ANSWERS.

Who can participate in Festiverse'26?+
Any student — from any college or university — can register and compete. Whether you're a solo performer or part of a team, all disciplines and skill levels are welcome.
When and where is the festival?+
Festiverse'26 takes place April 8–10, 2026 at Grand Orion Arena, GEC Samastipur. Doors open at 9 AM each day.
How do I register for an event?+
Hit "Register Now", create your account, pick your events, and complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your QR entry pass.
Are there accommodation facilities?+
Yes! Outstation participants can request on-campus accommodation at a nominal fee. Availability is limited so book early.
How are prizes distributed?+
Prizes are awarded on the closing night (April 10). Winners receive cash transfers within 7 working days. Trophies and certificates are handed out on stage.
